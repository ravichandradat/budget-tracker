One-time setup (5 minutes):
1. Go to script.google.com and click New Project
2. Delete any existing code, paste in the contents of google_apps_script.js
3. Click Deploy → New deployment → Web app
4. Set Execute as: Me  |  Who has access: Anyone
5. Click Deploy, authorize it, then copy the Web App URL
6. Paste that URL below

🔒 Your URL is saved in your browser. On a new device, just open this page and paste the same URL to load your data.
